Remote Mapping Projects

Open Roads, Philippines

Improved Real Time Decision making of Infrastructure Investments for the Philippines by linking geospatial road network data with rich geo-tagged social data collected through mobile phones. Imagery service provided by Imagery to the Crowd.

Nepal Earthquake

Over 5,000 volunteer mappers have improved the basemap of Nepal after the recent Earthquake. There's still some projects set up by HOT with tiles that need to be completed. Imagery service provided by Bing.

Projects: Solukhumbu, Tanahu, and Palpa
