Many websites offer points of entry and training instructions for people interested in getting involved in humanitarian mapping initiatives. This site provides one path. Follow our step-by-step instructions in this order:

  1. Set up an account and get a log in from OpenStreetMap

    Estimated time investment: 5-10 minutes.
    You must join OpenStreetMap in order to be able to map.

  2. Learn to map with us. After you have confirmed your OSM account and opened the first map, return here. Watch the video and follow the written instructions.

    Estimated time investment: 2 hours - 2 days.

    1. We will walk you through the process of mapping.
    2. You will practice mapping in your neighborhood.
